Buffalo Mac and Cheese

Buffalo Mac and Cheese is created when you combine 3 of the greatest foods of all time. Mac and cheese, chicken, and good old buffalo sauce. You really can't go wrong with any of these on their own. When combined, they create an amazing dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 package elbow macaroni
  • 1 roasted chicken
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 6 tablespoons flour
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 pinch ground black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce
  • 1/2 cup crumbled gorgonzola cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water for 8 minutes.
  2. Shred chicken meat into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Melt butter and whisk in flour until a thick paste forms.
  4. Pour in milk, whisk constantly for about 5 minutes. Cook until sauce is smooth.
  5. Stir cheddar and monterey jack into sauce until melted and combined.
  6. Stir in hot sauce.
  7. Add gorgonzola, chicken, and macaroni; mix well to combine.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
