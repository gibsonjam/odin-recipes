Cranberry Oatmeal Cookies

Oatmeal raisin cookies were my absolute favorite cookies from my childhood. When I got to college, my school cafeteria had cranberry raisin cookies. Those cookies blew my mind and quickly became my favorite thing in the place.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 1/2 cups sweetened dried cranberries

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat together the butter and sugars until light and fluffy. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating until combined after each addition. Beat in the vanilla until just combined.
  4. With the mixer on low, add the flour mixture until just combined. Scrape down the bowl. Beat in the oats and cranberries until just combined.
  5. Using a spoon, scoop the dough onto the lined baking sheets, about 1.5 inches apart. Bake 1 tray at a time until lightly browned, about 10 to 12 minutes. Let cool for 2 minutes.
